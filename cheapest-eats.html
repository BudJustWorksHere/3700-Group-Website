Discover the more affordable restraunts near Seattle Universiity

By Jaden Wong
Published May 16th, 2024

The ultimate guide to the best budget-friendly dining in the Emerald City. Whether you're a student or a visitor, discover hidden gems that offer delicious meals without breaking the bank. From food trucks serving mouth-watering tacos to cozy cafes with hearty breakfast options, we cover a variety of cuisines and dining experiences. Each recommendation is personally vetted for taste, value, and quality, ensuring you enjoy great food at wallet-friendly prices. Join us on a culinary journey through Seattle's diverse and affordable food scene, where every meal is a delightful bargain.

Dave's Hot Chicken

Dave's Hot Chicken is a fast-casual restaurant chain that has quickly gained a cult following for its Nashville-style hot chicken. Founded in 2017 in Los Angeles, California, by best friends Dave Kopushyan, Arman Oganesyan, and brothers Tommy and Gary Rubenyan Dave's Hot Chicken is a fast-casual restaurant chain that has quickly gained a cult following for its Nashville-style hot chicken. Founded in 2017 in Los Angeles, California, by best friends Dave Kopushyan, Arman Oganesyan, and brothers Tommy and Gary Rubenyan, the brand started as a humble pop-up in a parking lot. The overwhelming popularity of their spicy, flavorful chicken led to rapid expansion, with numerous locations opening across the United States and beyond. The star of the menu at Dave's Hot Chicken is, unsurprisingly, the hot chicken, which is served in various forms including tenders and sliders. Customers can customize their heat level, choosing from seven options ranging from "No Spice" to the fiery "Reaper," which is made with the Carolina Reaper pepper, one of the hottest peppers in the world. This allows diners to tailor their meal to their personal spice tolerance, making it accessible to a wide audience.

Seoul Bowl

Seoul Bowl offers a vibrant and modern twist on traditional Korean cuisine, serving up flavorful and customizable bowls that are both healthy and satisfying. Each bowl starts with a base of either rice, noodles, or greens, and is then topped with a choice of proteins such as Korean BBQ beef, Seoul Bowl offers a vibrant and modern twist on traditional Korean cuisine, serving up flavorful and customizable bowls that are both healthy and satisfying. Each bowl starts with a base of either rice, noodles, or greens, and is then topped with a choice of proteins such as Korean BBQ beef, spicy pork, or tofu. Fresh vegetables, pickled toppings, and signature sauces like gochujang and sesame vinaigrette add layers of taste and texture. The result is a colorful, balanced meal that caters to a variety of dietary preferences. With a focus on quality ingredients and authentic flavors, Seoul Bowl provides a delightful and convenient dining option for those seeking a taste of Korea in a fast-casual setting.

Katsu Burger

Katsu Burger is a unique fusion restaurant that combines the best of Japanese katsu and American fast food. Known for its massive, flavor-packed burgers, Katsu Burger features deep-fried, panko-crusted cutlets of chicken, pork, Katsu Burger is a unique fusion restaurant that combines the best of Japanese katsu and American fast food. Known for its massive, flavor-packed burgers, Katsu Burger features deep-fried, panko-crusted cutlets of chicken, pork, or tofu as the star of each sandwich. These crispy cutlets are paired with fresh vegetables, tangy pickles, and house-made sauces, all nestled within a soft sesame seed bun. Popular items include the Tokyo Classic, featuring tonkatsu sauce and Japanese mayo, and the Nori Burger, with a seaweed twist. For those with a hearty appetite, Katsu Burger also offers loaded fries and milkshakes, making it a go-to spot for a satisfying and indulgent meal.
